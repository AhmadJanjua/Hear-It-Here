What is Hear It Here?

Hear it here is a website for music lovers who want to share their opinions and discover new sounds. You can join different forums based on your favorite genres, artists, albums or songs, and chat with other users who have similar tastes. You can also use the genre match feature, which is like Tinder for music. You can swipe left or right on albums of different genres that are randomly selected for you, and get a personalized recommendation of a genre that suits your preferences. Hear it here is a fun and easy way to expand your musical horizons and connect with other fans.
